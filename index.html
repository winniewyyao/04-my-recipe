HotPot | HuoGuo

Ingredient

Soup Base

  • Beef Tallow Hot Pot Soup Base
  • Japanese Kombu Stock Powder

Hot Pot Ingredient

  • Napa Cabbage
  • Tong Ho (chrysanthemum greens)
  • Enoki Mushroom
  • Sliced Meat
  • Tofu
  • Frozen Meat Ball
  • Udon Noodles
  • Any other ingredients you like!

Direction

  1. Boil a pot of water.
  2. Put two kinds of soup base in the boiled water and stir it well.
  3. Add vegetables, meat slices and other ingredients. Wait until they are well-cooked.
  4. Add ingredients anytime when eating!
